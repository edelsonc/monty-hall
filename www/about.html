The Monty Hall problem is a well known puzzle in statistics. Based on the show "Let's Make a Deal", the Monty Hall problem presents the contestant with three identacle doors. They're told that two of the doors hide goats, while the final doors has a car behind it. The contestant is then prompted to select a door. One of the two remaining doors is then opened to reveal a goat. The contestant is then asked if they wish to keep their door or switch to the remaining unopen door.

Which would you choose?
